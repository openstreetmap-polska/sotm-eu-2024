How to get there?

State of the Map Europe 2024 will take place in the ‘Alchemium’ building of the Łódź University of Technology.

Train tickets can be purchased on the ‘PKP Intercity‘ website or the ‘KOLEO’ website.

Łódź is located in the heart of Poland. There are several ways to get to the city: directly to Łódź by plane, or you can also fly to another Polish city that is close to Łódź (Warsaw, Poznań or Gdańsk) and from there get to Łódź by train or bus.

Train tickets can be purchased on the PKP Intercity website or the KOLEO website

You may save money to fly to Warsaw, Poznań or Gdańsk due to cheaper plane tickets to these cities (low costs fly to these cities).

‘Jakdojade’ application is also available in the Play Market and Apple Store

In Łódź you can travel by trams and buses. Information about prices and routes can be checked on the website of the public transport management in Łódź. You can also check the directions in the ‘Jakdojade’ application

The application is also available in the Play Market and Apple Store
