Program

State of the Map Europe 2024 event will take place in Łódź at the Lodz University of Technology on July 18–21, 2024.

Event

Registration for the event starts on Thursday, July 18 at 5 p.m. at the Łódź University of Technology. There will be a welcome dinner at 6:00 p.m., and an evening walk around Łódź at 7:00 p.m.
